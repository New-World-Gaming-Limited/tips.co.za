Code of ethics

The principles that guide everything we publish.

Accuracy

Every factual claim on Tips must be verifiable. Bonus amounts, T&Cs, licence numbers, payment processing times, and minimum deposits are checked against operator websites before publication. When terms change, we update the relevant pages within 48 hours of becoming aware.

If a reader or operator reports an error, we investigate and correct it. All corrections are logged on our complaints and corrections page.

Independence

Tips earns revenue through affiliate commissions. No operator can pay to change a rating, influence a review outcome, or preview content before publication. Editorial scores are calculated using a consistent weighted formula disclosed on our methodology page.

Featured placement arrangements exist with a small number of operators and are always disclosed. These commercial relationships are handled separately from editorial decisions.

Transparency

We disclose affiliate relationships on every page through a visible bar above the header. Featured operators are identified where they appear. Our scoring methodology, including category weights, is published and accessible to readers.

Responsible gambling

Tips does not encourage gambling. We provide information to help South Africans make informed decisions about legal betting options. Every page on this site includes:

  • An 18+ age restriction notice
  • The SA Responsible Gambling Foundation helpline number (0800 006 008)
  • Links to responsible gambling resources

We do not publish content that targets minors, glorifies excessive gambling, or downplays risk. Bonus promotions are presented alongside their full terms and conditions, including wagering requirements.

Legal compliance

Tips only lists operators that hold a valid licence from a South African provincial gambling board. We verify licences using publicly available information from the relevant gambling boards. If an operator's licence is revoked or suspended, we remove them from the site.

We distinguish clearly between legal sports betting (permitted under the National Gambling Act) and online casino gambling (not currently licensed for remote play in South Africa).

No AI-generated content

All reviews, guides, and editorial content are written and fact-checked by human editors. AI tools may assist with research and data processing, but final editorial decisions and published text are the responsibility of named authors.

Reader complaints

We take complaints seriously. If you believe any content on Tips violates these standards, contact us at help@tips.co.za or submit a report through our complaints page. We respond to all complaints within 5 business days.
