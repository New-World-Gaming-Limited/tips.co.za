Frequently asked questions

Are all these betting sites legal in South Africa?

Yes. Every operator listed holds a licence from a South African provincial gambling board (Gauteng, Western Cape, KwaZulu-Natal, or another province). Online sports betting is legal in South Africa under the National Gambling Act.

Gbets currently offers the highest combined package: a R50 free bet on sign-up plus a 100% deposit match up to R5,000 (code GFWETS). For the best no-deposit offer, Easybet gives R50 free with no deposit required.

How fast are withdrawals?

Most licensed bookmakers process withdrawals within 24-72 hours. Betway and Hollywoodbets are typically fastest at 24-48 hours. Using Ozow or Capitec Pay often speeds up the process compared to bank transfers.
