What makes a good betting app?

Load speed and stability

An app that crashes during live betting or takes more than 3 seconds to load a market is not fit for purpose. Betway and Hollywoodbets both perform well on this measure in our testing under 4G and LTE conditions. Speed matters especially when you want to place an in-play bet quickly.

Bet slip reliability

The bet slip should hold your selections when you switch between tabs, not clear them when odds change. Several apps lose selections if a price shifts before you confirm. Betway handles this best by showing an "odds changed" notice and asking for confirmation rather than wiping the slip.

Deposit and withdrawal from the app

Every app on this list allows deposits through at least one instant local method. Withdrawals can usually be requested from within the app, though some bookmakers still redirect you to a browser for cashout. Hollywoodbets, Betway and Gbets all handle withdrawals natively in the app.

Android APK downloads

Several South African bookmakers are not available on Google Play but offer APK downloads from their official website. Easybet, Supabets and TicTacBets all use this route. Before installing an APK, you need to enable "unknown sources" in your Android security settings. Only download from the official bookmaker domain.

Frequently asked questions
