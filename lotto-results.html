How to play Lotto

Step 1: Choose numbers

Pick 6 numbers from 1 to 52, or select QuickPick for a random selection.

Step 2: Add Plus games

Optionally add Lotto Plus 1 (+R2.50) and Lotto Plus 2 (+R2.50) using the same numbers for two more chances.

Step 3: Buy your ticket

Purchase at any licensed retailer or online at nationallottery.co.za. Tickets close at 20:30 on draw days.

Step 4: Check results

Draws are broadcast on SABC and results appear online within minutes of the draw.

Frequently asked questions

Lotto draws take place on Wednesday and Saturday evenings. Results are typically available by 21:00 on draw days. Ticket sales close at 20:30.

Check results on the official National Lottery website at nationallottery.co.za, through the official Ithuba app, at licensed retailers, or on SABC television after each draw.

The Lotto bonus ball is a seventh number drawn after the six main numbers. It is used for Division 2 (5 main numbers + bonus) and Division 6 (3 main numbers + bonus). It is not drawn from a separate pool - it is an additional ball from the same 52-ball pool.

The jackpot odds are 1 in 20,358,520. The overall odds of winning any prize are approximately 1 in 38. Division 8 (2 main numbers + bonus) has the best odds at around 1 in 133.
