GrandWest in detail

GrandWest Casino & Entertainment World in Goodwood is the Western Cape's dominant casino resort. It is one of South Africa's largest gaming floors with 2,500+ slot machines and 80+ table games. The entertainment complex includes a 5,000-seat arena (Grand Arena) that hosts major concerts and sporting events, an ice rink, bowling alley, and over 30 restaurants and bars. The Park Inn by Radisson provides hotel accommodation on site. The venue is approximately 10 km from the Cape Town CBD and is accessible via N1/N2.

All Western Cape casinos operate under WCGRB regulation. Voluntary exclusion, deposit limits and counselling referrals are available at every venue. For immediate support, call 0800 006 008 (free, 24/7).
