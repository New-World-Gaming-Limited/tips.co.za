Gauteng's top casino resorts in detail

Montecasino (Fourways)

Montecasino is Tsogo Sun's flagship property and one of South Africa's most recognised entertainment destinations. Set in an Italian village-themed precinct in Fourways, Johannesburg, it draws over 5 million visitors a year. The gaming floor covers approximately 10,000 m² with 1,800+ slot machines and 60+ table games including blackjack, roulette and baccarat. The Palazzo Hotel is a 5-star property with 246 rooms. Entertainment includes the Teatro theatre (seats 2,000+), an IMAX cinema, bowling alley and over 45 restaurants. Full Montecasino guide.

Gold Reef City (Ormonde)

Gold Reef City is built on the site of a former gold mine in Ormonde, south of Johannesburg. The themed resort combines a casino with an amusement park. The casino features 1,700+ machines and 50+ table games. The adjacent theme park includes multiple rides and the Gold Reef City Hotel. The venue is particularly popular with families visiting the park while adults use the casino. Full Gold Reef City guide.

Emperors Palace (Kempton Park)

Emperors Palace is adjacent to OR Tambo International Airport, making it the most conveniently located casino for international visitors and transit passengers. Operated by Peermont Global, it has four on-site hotels including the 5-star Hotel Cascades and the 4-star Hotel Mondior. The gaming floor has 1,600+ slots and 90+ table games, one of the largest table game selections in Gauteng. The Kalahari Rain Theatre hosts regular shows.

Responsible gambling

All Gauteng casinos operate responsible gambling programmes under Gauteng Gambling Board regulations. Voluntary exclusion is available at every venue. If gambling is causing harm, call the SA Responsible Gambling Foundation: 0800 006 008 (free, 24/7).
