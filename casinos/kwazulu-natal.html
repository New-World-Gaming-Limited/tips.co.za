KZN's flagship casino resorts

Sibaya Casino & Entertainment Kingdom

Sibaya is Sun International's flagship KwaZulu-Natal property, located in Umdloti approximately 15 km north of Durban on the KZN North Coast. The resort has an African-themed design, 1,000+ slot machines and 50+ table games. The 4-star Sibaya Lodge offers hotel accommodation with ocean-facing rooms. Dining options include multiple restaurants and bars. Sibaya is well-positioned for visitors exploring the KZN North Coast.

Suncoast Casino & Entertainment

Suncoast occupies a prime position on Durban's Golden Mile beachfront. Operated by Tsogo Sun, the complex features 1,200+ slot machines and 50+ table games, a poker room and an extensive dining and entertainment precinct. The Suncoast Hotel Towers provide beachfront accommodation. The entertainment precinct includes a cinema, live music venues and restaurants along the promenade. The beachfront location makes Suncoast genuinely distinctive among South African casino resorts.

Responsible gambling

All KZN casinos operate responsible gambling programmes under KZNGBB regulation. Voluntary exclusion lists, trained floor staff and referral services are all required. For support, call the SA Responsible Gambling Foundation: 0800 006 008 (free, 24/7).
