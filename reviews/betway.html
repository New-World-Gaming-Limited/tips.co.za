Overview

Betway is a South African licensed bookmaker established in 2006, regulated by the Western Cape Gambling and Racing Board. The platform offers Casino, Live Casino, Esports in addition to its core sports betting product. Betway, as a licensee of the Western Cape Gambling and Racing Board, is required to provide responsible gambling tools including deposit limits, cooling-off periods and self-exclusion.
