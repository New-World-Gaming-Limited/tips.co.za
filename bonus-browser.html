Frequently asked questions

What is the best SA betting bonus right now?

World Sports Betting offers the largest headline figure at 100% up to R20,000, but this requires a sizable deposit to reach. For most bettors, Easybet's R50 no-deposit bonus combined with a 150% match up to R1,500 gives the best practical value from day one. Gbets also offers a strong combined package. Use the filter above to narrow by what matters most to you.

What does "no-deposit bonus" mean?

A no-deposit bonus is awarded on sign-up without putting any money in. Operators use them to let new users try the platform risk-free. In South Africa, Easybet, Hollywoodbets, Betfred, Supabets, YesPlay, Gbets, TicTacBets, ZARbet, and PlayTsogo all offer some form of no-deposit reward. Read the T&Cs carefully, as many require a wagering multiple before you can withdraw.

What is a wagering requirement?

A wagering requirement is the number of times you must bet through the bonus before withdrawing winnings. A 3x requirement on a R100 bonus means you must place R300 in bets. Lower numbers (1x, 3x) are more generous. Higher numbers (8x, 25x) make the bonus harder to convert to real cash. Hollywoodbets has one of the friendliest at 1x, while 10bet's 8x requirement is demanding.

Are all the operators listed here licensed in South Africa?

Yes. Every operator on this page holds a licence from a South African provincial gambling board, including the Western Cape, Gauteng, KwaZulu-Natal, Eastern Cape, and Mpumalanga regulators. We do not list offshore or unlicensed operators. You can verify any licence through the relevant board's public register.

Which SA bookmakers accept Capitec Pay?

Capitec Pay is accepted at Easybet, Betway, Hollywoodbets, Supabets, YesPlay, Gbets, and World Sports Betting. Use the "Capitec Pay" filter above to see only those operators instantly. Minimum deposits via Capitec Pay typically start at R10, with Gbets requiring a R25 minimum.

Can I claim more than one bonus?

Each operator's welcome bonus applies once per customer. You can sign up at multiple licensed bookmakers and claim a welcome bonus at each. However, creating multiple accounts at the same operator to claim a bonus more than once is against T&Cs and can result in account closure. Ongoing promotions like cashback and reload bonuses can be claimed repeatedly, subject to each operator's schedule.

What does the promo code do?

A promo code is entered during sign-up or deposit to activate a specific bonus. Some operators require a code; others apply the bonus automatically. The code shown on each card above is the one needed to claim the listed bonus. Copy it before you click "Visit Site" so you have it ready during registration.
