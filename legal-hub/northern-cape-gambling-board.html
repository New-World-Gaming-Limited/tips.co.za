Powers and Functions

Regulates gambling and liquor in the Northern Cape. Issues licences for casinos, bookmakers, and LPMs. The new 2024 Act combined gambling and liquor regulation under one authority.

How to Check a Bookmaker's Licence

Contact the NCGB at 053 839 3800 or info@ncgb.co.za to verify a bookmaker's licence.

How to File a Complaint

Email info@ncgb.co.za or call 053 839 3800. Provide the operator name, your account details, and a description of the issue.

Recent Developments

The Northern Cape passed the Gambling and Liquor Act 6 of 2024, combining its gambling and liquor regulation frameworks under a single new Act. TicTacBets holds a Northern Cape licence.
