How to Check a Bookmaker's Licence

The GGB does not publish a searchable online register. Contact them directly at (011) 581 4800 or info@ggb.org.za to verify a bookmaker's licence status. Licensed operators must display their licence certificate on their platform.

How to File a Complaint

Email info@ggb.org.za or call (011) 581 4800. Include the operator name, your account details, a description of the dispute, and any supporting evidence. The GGB will investigate and may institute disciplinary proceedings.

Recent Developments

In October 2025, the Supreme Court of Appeal ruled that Gauteng bookmakers cannot offer fixed-odds bets on casino games such as roulette. This landmark Portapa/Supabets decision confirmed that bookmaker licences are limited to sporting events under Gauteng's Gambling Act.
