Powers and Functions

Regulates all gambling in North West. Issues licences for casinos, bookmakers, and LPMs. Conducts compliance inspections and enforcement. North West is home to the iconic Sun City resort.

How to Check a Bookmaker's Licence

Contact the NWGB at 018 384 7766 or info@nwgb.org.za to verify licence status.

How to File a Complaint

Email info@nwgb.org.za or call 018 384 7766. The NWGB handles complaints against licensed gambling operators in the province.

Recent Developments

The NWGB has been conducting operations to destroy illegal gambling machines in the province, working with SAPS to suppress unlicensed gambling activity.
