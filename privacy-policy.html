Tips processes personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA). The legal bases for processing are:

5. Cookies

This site uses cookies for analytics (measuring traffic) and affiliate tracking (identifying clicks to bookmaker partners). Affiliate tracking cookies are used to attribute commission when a reader signs up at a bookmaker via a link on this site. You may disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to read this site.

6. Third-party services

This site uses Google Analytics for traffic measurement. Bookmaker links may contain affiliate tracking parameters operated by third-party affiliate networks. Each bookmaker has its own privacy policy governing how it handles data once you visit their site.

7. Data retention

Contact form submissions are retained for 12 months. Analytics data is retained in aggregate form. If you request deletion of your personal data, contact us at help@tips.co.za.

8. Your rights under POPIA

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact help@tips.co.za. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.
