How we score each bookmaker

Licensing and safety (25%)

Valid provincial licence, visible registration number, clear ownership, accessible complaint route and FICA-compliant KYC process.

Banking and payouts (20%)

Number of local payment methods accepted, withdrawal speed in our test, KYC document processing time and payout reliability.

Bonus quality (15%)

Usable value after wagering requirement, minimum odds restriction realism, deposit threshold affordability and withdrawal cap fairness.

Sport variety (15%)

Number of sports and leagues covered, market depth per game, bet builder availability and South African local sport coverage.

App and mobile (10%)

Android and iOS app quality, mobile website speed, registration flow, deposit and withdrawal from mobile.

Live betting (10%)

Number of in-play markets, odds update speed, live streaming availability and live cash-out reliability.

Customer support (5%)

Channel availability, response time testing, WhatsApp support and branch office access.
