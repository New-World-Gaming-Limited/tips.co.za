Frequently asked questions

Daily Lotto is drawn every day of the year, including weekends and public holidays. The draw takes place in the evening. Ticket sales close at 20:30 each day.

Pick 5 numbers from 1 to 36. Each board costs R3. You win the jackpot by matching all 5 numbers. There are 5 prize divisions based on how many numbers you match.

The jackpot odds for Daily Lotto are 1 in 376,992, making it the easiest National Lottery game to win the top prize. The smaller pool (1-36) and fewer numbers to match (5 instead of 6) account for this.
