Are no-deposit bonuses worth claiming?

The short answer is yes, when the terms are achievable. Hollywoodbets' R25 free bet is easy to use: 1x wagering at 1.50 odds means you need to place a single bet on an event priced at 1.50 or above. That is almost any market. The 24-hour expiry is tight but manageable if you know which match you want to bet on first.

Betfred's R50 free bet requires 3x wagering at 3.0 odds, which is more demanding. You need to bet R150 in total at minimum 3.0 odds before withdrawing. That means betting on markets where an outcome pays R3 or more for every R1 staked. Possible, but it narrows your options.

Easybet's R50 at 3x wagering with only 3/10 (1.30) minimum odds is generous. You can use almost any market to clear the turnover, including short-priced favourites. That makes it one of the most accessible free bets in the market.
