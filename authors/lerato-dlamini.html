About Lerato

Lerato tests every bookmaker listed on Tips through the full user journey: registration, deposit, betting, and withdrawal. She verifies bonus terms against operator websites and flags discrepancies. She fact-checks all reviews before publication.

Her process for each operator review involves opening a real funded account, placing at least three different bet types, testing customer support, and completing a withdrawal. She records times, screenshots T&C pages, and cross-references marketing claims against the operator's live site.

Lerato has identified over 40 bonus discrepancies between what operators advertise and what their actual terms state. When she finds one, the operator is contacted for comment before publication, and the finding is documented in the review.
