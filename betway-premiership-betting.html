About the Betway Premiership

The Betway Premiership is South Africa's top professional football division, run by the Premier Soccer League (PSL). It features 16 clubs competing in a double round-robin format from August to May. The league is affiliated to CAF (Confederation of African Football) and the FIFA system, with the champion entering the CAF Champions League.

Key clubs

  • Mamelodi Sundowns (most titles; Tshwane)
  • Kaizer Chiefs (largest fanbase; Johannesburg)
  • Orlando Pirates (oldest club; Johannesburg)
  • Cape Town City (Cape Town)
  • SuperSport United (Tshwane)
  • Stellenbosch FC (Cape Winelands)

PSL betting markets

  • Match Winner (1X2)
  • Both Teams to Score
  • Over/Under Goals
  • Correct Score
  • First Goalscorer
  • League Outright Winner
  • Top Goalscorer Outright

Live betting on PSL matches

All 44 licensed SA bookmakers offer live in-play betting on Betway Premiership matches. Betway, as league sponsor, updates odds most rapidly and provides the widest in-play market range. Odds move quickly during PSL matches - especially around the Soweto Derby (Chiefs vs Pirates) and Tshwane Derby (Sundowns vs SuperSport United), which attract the highest betting volumes in the PSL calendar.
